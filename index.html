About Us

Driven by our philosophy of delivering quality & consistency in laid back surroundings to create effortless style, Ludlow Blunt blends a heritage barbershop narrative with modern day techniques & craftsmanship. Adapting both men's & women's classical styles with contemporary appeal, we aim to create both in hair & product design a refreshing take on the past while anticipating the new.
